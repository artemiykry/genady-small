# TeamTree

Hierarchy of teams.

- **Purpose** — orient on the org; navigate by structure.
- **Shows** — collapsible tree of teams; member count per node.
- **Actions** — `create_team`, `rename_team`, `reparent_team` (drag-drop); open `TeamPage`.
- **States**
  - *collapsed* — top-level teams only.
  - *expanded* — full hierarchy.
- **Composed of** — team node.
