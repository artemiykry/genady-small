# Calendar

Time as a first-class surface. The calendar shows events from your external calendars, enriched with local annotations and links into the rest of the graph.

## Sources

- Google Calendar
- CalDAV
- Outlook

## What you can do with an event

- Open or auto-create a [meeting note](meetings.md) for it.
- Link it to a task, doc, person, incident.
- Add private annotations that never sync out.

## Views

- Daily view
- Weekly view
- Monthly view

## What lives elsewhere

- *What was said in the meeting* → [meetings](meetings.md)
- *Work, and when you plan to do it* → [tasks](tasks.md)
